About College

NMAMIT, also known as Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology, is a constituent college of Nitte (Deemed to be University). It's located in Nitte, Karkala Taluk, Udupi district, Karnataka. The institute offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including engineering and computer applications.
